COVID-19 Guidelines

Precautions

1. Clean your hands often.

2. Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible.

3. Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.

4. Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth.

5. Stay home if you feel unwell.

Symptoms

1. Fever or Cough

2. New loss of taste or smell

3. Congestion or runny nose

4. Muscle or body aches

5. Difficulty in breathing

6. Headache

7. Nausea or vomiting

Emergency Attention

1. Trouble breathing

2. Persistent pain or pressure in the chest

3. Inability to wake or stay awake

4. Bluish lips or face

5. New Confusion

6. High Fever
